About PMM Day

The 1st Annual PMM Day/weekend is a weekend of events showcasing the family portion of the long standing Miles College Purple Maching Machine and their family and friends. The main purpose of this event is to create a tradition that bridges the gaps between old-school band members, those that marched under the direction of Mr. Arthur Means from 1996-2006, the more current band members, those that marched under the direction of Mr. Donald Crawford from 2007-2012 and/or Mr.James Merriweather from 2012-2016, and the current members of the Purple Marching Machine, under the direction of Mr. Willie Snipes.

Meet & Greet

MEET & GREET
Friday, 4/12 location: Sleek Sports Bar 1005 20th St S, Birmingham, AL 35205

This is the first day of the PMM Weekend and it begins with a meet & greet. This is an opportunity for new and old members to meet up and see each other before the big saturday picnic. This event is ideal for those who are coming in from out of town and those that just want to hang with other band members and fellowship.

This event is a adult only event and will include alcohol. You are not welcome to bring drugs to the venue and police will be used for our security.

PMM Picnic

PMM Picnic
Saturday, 4/13 Location: Fairfield City Park

The first annual PMM Picnic will be held on a Saturday at Fairfield City Park. We will have an opening booth when you get to the park to grab you wrist band for those that pre-ordered and a line for those who need a wristband. You must pay the entry fee in order to participate in the picnic. Food and drinks will only be given to those that possess one of the wrist band colors(white, purple, or gold)

Please be mindful of those that have volunteered to make sure this event is a success so please be patient when it comes to waiting on food and drinks. We wanted the PMM Family to not be restained to serving so everything is catered by a vendor. There is also a 4 drink limit but you are more than welcome to bring your own beverages as well to the picnic.
